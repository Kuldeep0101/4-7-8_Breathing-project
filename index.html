Health Benefits

Reduces Anxiety

The 4-7-8 technique activates the parasympathetic nervous system, helping to calm the mind and reduce feelings of stress and anxiety.

Improves Sleep

Regular practice can help you fall asleep faster and enjoy deeper, more restorative sleep by promoting relaxation.

Lowers Blood Pressure

Deep breathing exercises have been shown to reduce blood pressure and heart rate, supporting cardiovascular health.

How it works:

  1. Inhale through your nose for 4 seconds
  2. Hold your breath for 7 seconds
  3. Exhale through your mouth for 8 seconds
  4. Repeat this cycle 3-4 times for maximum benefit
